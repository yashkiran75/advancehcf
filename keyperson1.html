DR. SAURABH KOLE

NGO Director

He has been working in the field of Critical Care Medicine and Emergency Medicine
for more than 25 years, and is Chairman - Indian Society of Critical Care Medicine, Eastern zone.

His active contribution in treating disaster-hit patients is well-known, and
he has been part of the Doctors' Team in various capacities for innumerable national
and international disasters.

His interest in making people aware of organ donation and organ transplant
has been well-acknowledged, making him the
General Secretory- Organ Donation (Indian Medical Association).

He is presently working as the Senior Cardiologist
and is the ITU incharge of Bellevue Hospital, Kolkata.
